Discover Taman Ekologi Paya Bakau Kinabenuwa

Explore the beauty of the protected mangrove ecosystem of Taman Ekologi Paya Bakau Kinabenuwa, a crucial ecological sanctuary in Labuan, Malaysia.

Know about the ecosystem before you visit

Featured Places in Taman Ekologi Paya Bakau Kinabenuwa

Mangrove Forest

A thriving mangrove forest ecosystem serves as a critical barrier for coastal protection and a habitat for diverse wildlife.

Wildlife Sanctuary

Home to over 200 species of wildlife, including rare birds, marine creatures, and diverse plant species in the mangrove area.

Local Fishing Community

The mangrove area supports the local fishermen who practice sustainable fishing methods and contribute to preserving the environment.
