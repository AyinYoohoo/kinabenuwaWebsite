our story

15 Years of Experience

Taman Ekologi Paya Bakau Kinebenuwa, located in Labuan, Malaysia, is a preserved mangrove forest that serves as a natural habitat for a rich variety of wildlife. The ecosystem is home to various species of plants, fish, and birds, playing a crucial role in coastal protection, biodiversity conservation, and mitigating climate change impacts.

Our mission is to ensure the sustainability of this unique ecosystem by raising awareness about the importance of mangroves and promoting responsible tourism practices. Through continuous research, conservation efforts, and community engagement, we aim to protect and restore this valuable natural resource.
